EXERCISES
PUSH
1Pike push-ups Shoulders (front deltoids), triceps (long head), upper trapezius
From a push-up, walk your feet in until your hips pike up into an upside-down V. Bend your elbows to lower your head toward the floor between your hands, then press back to straight arms.
Cue: hips high, let the shoulders press.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x12.
1, 5Decline push-ups Upper chest (clavicular head), front deltoids, triceps
Feet up on a chair, hands slightly wider than your shoulders, body in a straight downhill line. Lower your chest to an inch off the floor, then press up. Higher feet works the upper chest more.
Cue: one straight line, chest to the floor.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x15.
1Push-ups Chest (sternal head), front deltoids, triceps, serratus anterior
Hands slightly wider than your shoulders under your upper chest, body in one line. Lower over about two seconds until your chest nearly touches, then press straight up.
Cue: push the floor away, stay tight.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x20.
1Bench dips Lower chest, triceps
Hands on a chair edge beside your hips, butt off the front, legs out in front. Bend your elbows straight back to about 90 degrees, then press back up to straight arms.
Cue: shoulders down, no shrug.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x12.
1, 5Lateral raises (against a doorframe) Side deltoids
Grip a doorframe low with a straight arm, feet at its base. Lean your body out to the side, then pull yourself upright by driving through the side of your shoulder. Switch sides.
Cue: fall out, pull back from the shoulder.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x12 per side.
PULL
2, 5Pull-ups & chin-ups Lats, biceps, grip (a chin-up grip works the biceps more)
Hang from the bar at full stretch, palms away for pull-ups or toward you for chin-ups. Pull your chin over the bar, then lower all the way to a dead hang.
Cue: chest to bar, no swing.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x10.
2, 5Inverted rows Upper back, rhomboids, rear deltoids, biceps
Hang under a low bar or table edge, body straight and heels down. Pull your chest to the edge with your elbows wide, then lower under control. More horizontal is harder.
Cue: pinch the shoulder blades.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x15.
POSTURE
2Prone Y-T-W raises Mid and lower trapezius, rhomboids, rear deltoids
Face down, arms overhead, thumbs up. Lift the arms into a Y, then a T, then a W (elbows to your ribs), starting each lift from the shoulder blades.
Cue: lift from the blades, not the neck.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x10 each shape.
2, 4Superman Lower back (erector spinae), glutes
Face down, arms out front. Lift your chest, arms, and legs a few inches, reaching long; squeeze, then lower slowly. Keep looking at the floor.
Cue: reach long, don't crunch.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x12.
2, 4Neck tucks & extensions Deep neck flexors and neck extensors
Tuck: glide your chin straight back into a double chin, hold, release. Extend: hands behind your head, gently press your head back into them. Alternate both directions.
Cue: slow and gentle, never force.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x10 each way.
CORE
2, 5Hanging knee raises Abs, hip flexors, grip
Hang with your shoulders pulled down. Raise your knees, then curl your pelvis up toward your ribs at the top. Lower slowly, no swinging.
Cue: curl the hips, don't swing.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x15.
2Side plank (held for time) Obliques, deep core, side glutes
Elbow under your shoulder, feet stacked. Lift your hips into one straight line from head to heels and hold, top hip stacked over the bottom.
Cue: hips high, don't sag.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x45s per side.
LEGS
4, 5Split squats Quads, glutes
Long staggered stance. Lower straight down until your back knee nearly touches, then drive up through your front foot, front knee over your toes.
Cue: straight down, drive the front foot.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x15 per leg.
4, 5Single-leg glute bridge Glutes, hamstrings
On your back, one foot planted close, other leg lifted. Drive your hips to full lockout through the planted heel, ribs down, then lower slowly.
Cue: all glute, no low back.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x15 per leg.
4Nordic hamstring curl Hamstrings (the knee-bending action)
Kneel with your heels anchored under a sofa, body straight from knees to head. Lower your torso as slowly as you can, then push off your hands back up.
Cue: fight the fall, go slow.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x6.
4Copenhagen plank Adductors (inner thigh), obliques
Side plank with your top inner thigh resting on a chair. Press it down and lift your hips straight, then raise the bottom leg to meet it. Easier: tuck the bottom leg under you.
Cue: crush the surface, hips up.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x20s per side.
1, 4Calf raises (on a stair) Calves (gastrocnemius and soleus)
Balls of your feet on a stair, heels hanging off. Drop your heels for a full stretch, then press up to full tiptoe. Do one set knees-bent for the soleus.
Cue: full stretch, full squeeze.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x20.
1, 4Tibialis raises (against a wall) Shin (tibialis anterior)
Back against a wall, heels a foot out. Keeping your heels down, lift your toes up toward your shins as high as you can, then lower slowly.
Cue: feel the shin burn.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3x20.
RUNNING
1Short run Legs, plus heart and lungs
Easy, conversational pace, about 1.5 miles, run after your lifting.
Cue: light, quick, tall.
lvl 1. Graduate: 1.5 mi under ~14 min.
3Long run Aerobic base
Slow, steady 3 miles, clearly slower than your short run, finishing relaxed. The easy pace is what builds the aerobic base.
Cue: easy enough to nose-breathe.
lvl 1. Graduate: 3 mi under ~30 min.
5Hill run Leg power, plus aerobic
Hilly route of about 1.5 miles. Short quick steps and a slight lean on the climbs; jog or walk the descents to recover.
Cue: push the ground away.
lvl 1. Graduate: 1.5 mi hills under ~16 min.
